Download Points (DP)

What is DP (Download Points)?
Download points is special points. With DP you can download any music from plug.dj (only from communities where Origem Bot is turned on)

How do I earn DP?
Visit community where is Origem Bot turned on and play some music (be DJ). For each 1 woot you receive 3 DP, for each 1 grab you receive 10 DP.

How much cost download one song? (MP3)
Song duration (seconds) * 0,09 = price. (5:00 song = 27 DP)
With "Playlist Downloader script" is price Song duration (seconds) * 0,4! (5:00 song = 120DP)

Okay.. and how to download MP3 from plug.dj?
Just write download command in community where is Origem Bot turned on. You can download only current playing song.
