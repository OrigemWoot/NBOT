Playlist Downloader

What is it?
With playlist downloader you can download your play-list(s) from PLUG.DJ

How to use playlist downloader?
Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar. Playlist downloader working only in Google Chrome

How much cost download one song? (MP3)
Song duration (seconds) * 0,4! (5:00 song = 120 DP)

More info what you must know
- Playlist downloader "eating" DP from NBOT. How I can earn DP?
- To check, how much DP you have, write dp command in any community where is NBOT
- When script finish downloading your playlist, you got a download link. You must download it fast. Link is available only 2 hours.
- I recommend downloading playlists at a time. Not two simultaneously. Script may fail and you must start again from the beginning.
- Sondcloud music isnt supported
- Max song limit per download is 35:00
- This is my first version of downloader, if you find bugs sorry :*
